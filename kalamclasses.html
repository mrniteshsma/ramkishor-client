About Kalam Classes Merta

My name is Ramkishor Sankhala, and I am from Merta Road. I am the founder and lead educator at Kalam Classes Merta. Established on July 7, 2022, Kalam Classes Merta has been a cornerstone of quality education in our community.

At Kalam Classes Merta, we are dedicated to nurturing young minds and fostering a passion for learning. Our institute provides comprehensive education solutions for students ranging from 1st class to 12th class. With a focus on academic excellence and holistic development, we aim to empower students to achieve their full potential.

As a professional educator, I specialize in teaching Physics, Chemistry, and Biology. While my primary expertise lies in these subjects, I also offer occasional classes in Mathematics. With years of experience and a commitment to excellence, I strive to create an engaging and supportive learning environment where every student can thrive.

At Kalam Classes Merta, we believe in the power of education to transform lives. Our mission is to inspire and empower the next generation of leaders, thinkers, and innovators. Join us on this journey of knowledge and discovery at Kalam Classes Merta, where learning knows no bounds.
